While in class, Wildan saw his friend drawing a train on paper, which inspired him to try drawing something similar. From that moment, Wildan began drawing anything that caught his interest. When he was at a train station, he would look closely at the bottom of the trains to study the shape of their wheels and other details.

One day, his mother bought him an encyclopedia about vehicles filled with fascinating pictures. What was originally just an ordinary book for people to read became a valuable reference for him. When Wildan later discovered that there is a profession dedicated to drawing, he became intrigued and set his sights on becoming one of them.
